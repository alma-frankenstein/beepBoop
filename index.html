Mr. Roboger's Neighborhood

Hello, children! Want to play a game?

Enter a number. We'll count from zero to that number. When we come to a number that has a '3', we'll say "won't you be my neighbor?". If the number doesn't have a '3' but it has a '2', we'll say "boop!". If it doesn't have a '3' or a '2' but it has a '1', we'll say "beep!". Fun!
